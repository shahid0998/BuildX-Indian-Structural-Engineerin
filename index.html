About BuildX

A Legacy of
Engineering
Excellence.

Buildx Indian Structural Engineering Private Limited is built upon a strong foundation of engineering excellence and integrity, originally laid by Mr. Rafiq Ahmad Wani in 1997. As a Registered Government Contractor, he successfully executed and delivered hundreds of projects across residential, commercial, institutional, and infrastructure sectors.

Proudly continuing this legacy, Er. Faizan Rafiq Wani and Er. Muyeen Rafiq Wani, both Structural Engineers, have been serving clients across Delhi NCR, Gurugram, and various regions across India since 2012 — combining advanced structural engineering expertise with extensive on-site experience.

Modern Technologies

Advanced Retrofitting
Solutions.

Our engineering team utilizes industry-leading rehabilitation technologies and proven methodologies to deliver lasting structural solutions.

⚡

CFRP Strengthening

Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer — lightweight, high-strength systems used to enhance the load-carrying capacity of beams, slabs, columns, and walls without adding significant weight.

🏛️

Concrete & Steel Jacketing

Proven strengthening techniques that add a new reinforced concrete or steel layer around existing members to improve structural performance, durability, and ductility.

💉

Epoxy Injection & Crack Repair

Advanced repair methods using high-strength epoxy resins to restore structural continuity, seal cracks, and prevent further deterioration and water ingress.

🛡️

Cathodic Protection Systems

Galvanic and impressed current cathodic protection solutions that significantly extend the service life of reinforced concrete structures exposed to corrosive environments.

🔬

Micro Concrete & Polymer Repairs

High-performance, non-shrink micro concrete and polymer-modified repair systems for restoring damaged concrete members and improving structural integrity.

🌊

Waterproofing & Protection

Advanced waterproofing membranes, crystalline coatings, and protective systems to prevent water ingress and environmental degradation of structural elements.

Our Methodology

How We Work.

01

Investigation & Assessment

Thorough site investigation, visual inspection, NDT testing, and structural health evaluation to understand the current condition and identify root causes of distress.

02

Root Cause Analysis

Detailed engineering analysis to determine the underlying causes of structural deterioration — corrosion, overloading, design deficiency, or environmental factors.

03

Design & Strategy

Development of the most effective rehabilitation strategy with detailed engineering design, material specifications, and methodology tailored to each project.

04

Execution & Quality Assurance

Expert execution with rigorous quality control, site supervision, and documentation to ensure every solution meets design intent and long-term performance goals.
