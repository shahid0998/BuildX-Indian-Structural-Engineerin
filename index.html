Our Methodology

How We Work.

01

Discovery & Scope

We conduct a thorough site visit, review existing data, and define clear project parameters in collaboration with your team.

02

Analysis & Design

Using ETABS, STAAD Pro, and ANSYS, our engineers develop structural models and optimize designs for safety and economy.

03

Documentation & Approval

Comprehensive drawing sets and calculation reports are prepared, meeting all relevant IS/IRC code requirements for statutory approval.

04

Site Support & Handover

Our team provides construction supervision, RFI resolution, and detailed as-built documentation through project completion.
